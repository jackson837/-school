Our History

September 2013

The school started with about 17 students in JSS1, 30 students in Primary 4 & 5, and 20 students in Nursery, operating from a 3‑bedroom flat with about four teaching staff.

Nov–Dec 2013

The first four downstairs classrooms were put in place to support our growing student body.

Sept–Oct 2016

The complementary four downstairs classrooms were added, completing the ground floor.

September 2017

The first floor of eight rooms was added, expanding our capacity and offerings.

2016 Rainy Season

We planted our first set of economic trees and flowers and built a fish pond. Today, our campus features a variety of plants—from cash crops and fruit trees to economic plants studied in Economics and Agriculture.
